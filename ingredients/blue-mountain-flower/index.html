Blue Mountain Flower

Where to find Blue Mountain Flower:

Blue Mountain Flowers are, as the name suggests, blue flowers. They look the same as purple and red mountain flowers, and can be found in large droves in the foothills and below the snowline in the mountains. A large collection of Blue Mountain Flowers can be found near Sleeping Tree Camp, southeast of Rorikstead, and they are frequently sold by alchemy vendors.
