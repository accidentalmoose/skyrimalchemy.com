Abecean Longfin

Where to find Abecean Longfin:

Abecean Longfin is a silvery fish that can be caught in lakes, ponds and rivers. A good way to find them is to look for dragonflies above the water: you can usually find a Longfin or two right beneath them. They can also be found in fish barrels or sold by alchemy vendors.
