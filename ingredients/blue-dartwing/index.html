Blue Dartwing

Where to find Blue Dartwing:

Blue Dartwings can be harvested by catching blue dragonflies. Dragonflies are normally found near water during the day. A large collection of dragonflies exists near The Apprentice Stone, on a island in the march west-northwest of Morthal.

Ideal ingredient matchers:

These are ingredients which have two or more effects in common with Blue Dartwing:
