Creep Cluster

Where to find Creep Cluster:

Creep Cluster are harvested from orange-colored roots growing on rocks. They're generally only found in Eastmarch, with a large collection found near Steamcrag Camp, south of Windhelm.

Ideal ingredient matchers:

These are ingredients which have two or more effects in common with Creep Cluster:

  • Dwarven Oil (Restore Magicka, Weakness to Magic)
  • Giant's Toe (Damage Stamina Regen, Fortify Carry Weight)
  • Scaly Pholiota (Fortify Carry Weight, Weakness to Magic)
  • Taproot (Restore Magicka, Weakness to Magic)
