Human Heart

Where to find Human Heart:

Human Hearts cannot be looted off dead humans, but are instead found where in locations where people have performed the black sacrement ritual to summon the Dark Brotherhood. Samples exist in the Aretino Residence in Windhelm, Black-Briar Manor in Riften, and Volunruud, southeast of Morthal
