Ashen Grass Pod

Where to find Ashen Grass Pod:

Added in the Dragonborn DLC, Ashen Grass Pod is harvested from spiky grass plants all over Solstheim. There is a sample of the pods downstairs in Severin Manor in Raven Rock.

Ideal ingredient matchers:
