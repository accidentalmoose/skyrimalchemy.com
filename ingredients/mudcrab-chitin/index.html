Mudcrab Chitin

Where to find Mudcrab Chitin:

Mudcrab Chitin are the claws of Mudcrabs and can be salvaged from their bodies. Mudcrabs can be found along riverbanks and lakeshores, and individual samples of Mudcrab Chitin can be found in Pale Imperial Camp, west of Dawnstar, and Riverside Shack, southwest of Windhelm.
