Boar Tusk

Where to find Boar Tusk:

Added in the Dragonborn DLC, Boar Tusks are long tusks dropped by Bristlebacks and the Rieklings that ride them. There are plenty of tusks in Bristleback Cave, south of Northshore Landing in Solstheim.

Ideal ingredient matchers:

None
