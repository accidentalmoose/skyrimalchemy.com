Torchbug Thorax

Where to find Torchbug Thorax:

Torchbug Thorax can be harvested by catching torchbugs. Torchbug can be found at night in non-snowy areas: their illuminated bodies make them easy to spot. A large collection of torchbugs exists near Talking Stone Giant Camp, northeast of Rorikstead along the Hjaal river.
