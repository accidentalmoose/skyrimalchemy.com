Emperor Parasol Moss

Where to find Emperor Parasol Moss:

Added in the Dragonborn DLC, Emperor Parasol Moss are plants growing from Emperor Parasol mushrooms. The mushrooms can be found near Tel Mithryn in southeastern Solstheim.

Ideal ingredient matchers:

These are ingredients which have two or more effects in common with Emperor Parasol Moss:

  • Red Mountain Flower (Damage Health, Fortify Magicka)
  • Troll Fat (Damage Health, Fortify Two-handed)
  • Void Salts (Damage Health, Fortify Magicka)
