Salmon Roe

Where to find Salmon Roe:

Added in the Hearthfire DLC, Salmon Roe look like small orange pearls on a wooden plate. They can be found in player-built homes or by harvesting jumping salmon. The waterfalls of White River are an especially good place to find jumping salmon.

Ideal ingredient matchers:

These are ingredients which have two or more effects in common with Salmon Roe:
