Pine Thrush Egg

Where to find Pine Thrush Egg:

Pine Thrush Eggs are rust-colored eggs that can be harvested from bird nests. Both Pine Thrush Eggs and Rock Warbler Eggs come from similar-looking nests, but Pine Thrush Eggs are more common in forest areas. Around Heartwood Mill, west of Riften, there's a large collection of Pine Thrush nests.
