Scathecraw

Where to find Scathecraw:

Added in the Dragonborn DLC, Scathecraw are dark purple plants that can be harvested. The plant can be found in Solstheim areas covered in ash. There are many plants downstairs in Ienth Farm in Raven Rock.

Ideal ingredient matchers:

These are ingredients which have two or more effects in common with Scathecraw:

  • Spawn Ash (Ravage Magicka, Ravage Stamina)
