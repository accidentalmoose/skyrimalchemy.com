Giant Lichen

Where to find Giant Lichen:

Giant Lichen are red-brown fungus found outside in the marshes of Hjaalmarch. The surroundings of Movarth's Lair, north of Morthal, has a large collection of Giant Lichen, as do the areas around Morthal itself.
