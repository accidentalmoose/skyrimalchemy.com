Scaly Pholiota

Where to find Scaly Pholiota:

Scaly Pholiota is a light yellow fungus that grow on tree stumps and fallen trees. They're common in The Rift forests, with the areas around Darklight Tower, southwest of Riften, and Honeystrand Cave to the south of Ivarstead, being especially good for finding Scaly Pholiota.
