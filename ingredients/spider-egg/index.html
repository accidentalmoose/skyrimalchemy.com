Spider Egg

Where to find Spider Egg:

Spider Eggs are yellow-green eggs that can be harvested from egg and web sacs of Frostbite Spiders. Spiders are generally found in dungeons, where eggs are more likely to appear, but can also spawn in the wilderness. There's a lot of Spider Eggs in Harmugstahl, northeast of Markarth, and in Ironbind Barrow, inbetween Winterhold and Whiterun.
