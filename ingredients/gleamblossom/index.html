Gleamblossom

Where to find Gleamblossom:

Added in the Dawnguard DLC, Gleamblossom is a purple and blue flower. They can be found in caves and other dark places; there are especially large collection of Gleamblossom in Darkfall Passage and Forgotten Vale, both of which can be accessed through Darkfall Cave, north of Markarth.
