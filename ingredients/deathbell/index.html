Deathbell

Where to find Deathbell:

Deathbells are large blue-colored flowers. They are commonly found in swampy areas, such as Hjaalmarch, with a large number of them in the Forelhost Refectory in the Forelhost ruin southest of Riften.

Ideal ingredient matchers:

These are ingredients which have two or more effects in common with Deathbell:

  • Poison Bloom (Damage Health, Slow)
  • River Betty (Damage Health, Slow)
  • Small Antlers (Damage Health, Weakness to Poison)
