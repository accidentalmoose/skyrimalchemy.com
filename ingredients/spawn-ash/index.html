Spawn Ash

Where to find Spawn Ash:

Added in the Dragonborn DLC, Spawn Ash is a grey substance in a tan bowl. It is normally dropped by Ash Spawn, which randomly spawn in ashy areas of Solstheim and can also be found in Raven Rock Temple and during the March of the Dead quest.

Ideal ingredient matchers:

These are ingredients which have two or more effects in common with Spawn Ash:

  • Bone Meal (Ravage Stamina, Resist Fire)
  • Scathecraw (Ravage Magicka, Ravage Stamina)
  • Snowberries (Fortify Enchanting, Resist Fire)
