Snowberries

Where to find Snowberries:

Snowberries are red berries that grow on the Snowberry bush. Snowberries can be found outdoors in the snowy areas of Skyrim, and are particularly prevalent in the surroundings of Bloodlet Throne, southwest of Helgen, Windward Ruins, southwest of Dawnstar and Driftshade Refuge, southeast of Dawnstar.
