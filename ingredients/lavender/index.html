Lavender

Where to find Lavender:

Lavender is a purple-colored herb that grows in large parts of Skyrim, but seem to be particularly common in Whiterun hold. Many samples exist in the city of Whiterun itself, and there are a lot Lavender around Chillwind Depths, south of Dragon Bridge, as well.
