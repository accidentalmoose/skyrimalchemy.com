Frost Mirriam

Where to find Frost Mirriam:

Frost Mirriam are dried plants found hanging in houses, often near garlic and elves ears. A couple of good examples of Frost Mirriam sources are Black-Briar Lodge east of Riften, Vlindrel Hall in Markarth and Burguk's Longhouse in Dushnikh Yal, east of Markarth.

Ideal ingredient matchers:

These are ingredients which have two or more effects in common with Frost Mirriam:
