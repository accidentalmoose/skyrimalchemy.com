Glowing Mushroom

Where to find Glowing Mushroom:

Glowing Mushroom are fungus growing on cave walls which are easy to spot because of their glow. A large collection of Glowing Mushrooms can be found in Tolvald's Cave, northwest of Shor's Stone in the Rift.
