Thistle Branch

Where to find Thistle Branch:

Thistle Branch is harvested from Thistle plants, which are spiky-looking plants with purple flowers. Thistle plants are especially common in the southern pine forests, but can be found everywhere in low elevations. Large collections of Thistle Branches can be found around Riverwood, Falkreath and Pinewatch, northeast of Falkreath.
