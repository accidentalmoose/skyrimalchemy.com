Briar Heart

Where to find Briar Heart:

Briar Hearts are dropped by Forsworn Briarhearts. Briarhearts can usually be found in Forsworn camps, such as Lost Valley Redoubt. There are also a couple of Briar Hearts in Gallows Rock.

Ideal ingredient matchers:

These are ingredients which have two or more effects in common with Briar Heart:

  • Ectoplasm (Fortify Magicka, Restore Magicka)
  • Human Flesh (Paralysis, Restore Magicka)
  • Pearl (Fortify Block, Restore Magicka)
  • Red Mountain Flower (Fortify Magicka, Restore Magicka)
  • Tundra Cotton (Fortify Block, Fortify Magicka)
