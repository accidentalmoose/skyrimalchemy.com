Namira's Rot

Where to find Namira's Rot:

Namira's Rot are mushrooms with brownish caps, which are commonly found on cave floors. The largest source of Namira's Rot is Chillwind Depths, a mountain cavern on the border of Hjaalmarch and The Reach.

Ideal ingredient matchers:

These are ingredients which have two or more effects in common with Namira's Rot:

  • Daedra Heart (Damage Magicka, Fear)
  • Gleamblossom (Fear, Regenerate Health)
  • Luna Moth Wing (Damage Magicka, Regenerate Health)
  • Nordic Barnacle (Damage Magicka, Regenerate Health)
