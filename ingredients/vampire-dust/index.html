Vampire Dust

Where to find Vampire Dust:

Vampire Dust is a dark grey substance inside a wooden bowl. They're always dropped by Vampires, which can be found in dark lairs and caves all around Skyrim, and sometimes in the wilderness as well. Nightcaller Temple outside of Dawnstar has a few samples of Vampire Dust, as do both The Mortar and Pestle shop inside of Dawnstar itself and Honeyside in Riften.
