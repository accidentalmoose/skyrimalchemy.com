Crimson Nirnroot

Where to find Crimson Nirnroot:

Crimson Nirnroot is a special type of Nirnroot found exclusively in Blackreach. Like normal Nirnroots, the crimson variant is usually found near water and makes a chiming sound when you get near it.

Ideal ingredient matchers:

These are ingredients which have two or more effects in common with Crimson Nirnroot:

  • Nirnroot (Damage Health, Damage Stamina, Invisibility, Resist Magic)
