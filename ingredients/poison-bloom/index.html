Poison Bloom

Where to find Poison Bloom:

Added in the Dawnguard DLC, Poison Bloom is a purple flower which can only be found in Darkfall Cave, north of Markarth. If not immediately picked, it will release a poisonous gas when someone is near.

Ideal ingredient matchers:

These are ingredients which have two or more effects in common with Poison Bloom:

  • Netch Jelly (Fear, Fortify Carry Weight)
  • River Betty (Damage Health, Fortify Carry Weight, Slow)
